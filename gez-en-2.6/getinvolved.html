Get Involved in the digital edition of the Lexicon Linguae Aethiopicae

If you feel you are qualified to edit the lexicon and would like to contribute, by adding new translations for example or fixing minor errors, you can request permission to edit.

Please email the editors highlighting what your competences and interests are and what you would like to contribute.

If you just want to report an issue of any kind, please use the orange button on the navigation bar, which will lead you to our github repository. Try to be as clear as possible and provide the link to the page where you found the issue.
